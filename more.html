Upload Cool Rides or Find Car & Bike Pictures


PakWheels.com is committed to giving platforms to automobile enthusiasts so that they can showcase their talent to people country-wide. One such platform which PakWheels.com is offering is ‘Cool Rides.' It is a platform where auto enthusiasts can share their favorite car or bike pictures to let people watch and get entertained without spending a penny. If you are an automobile lover and want to watch pictures of different unique and vintage vehicles or you want people to see what you have got under your sleeve then ‘Cool Rides’ is the place for you. The option is available to both car and bike users, either of them can upload the images of their respective vehicles with some details such as description. In simple terms the platform works same as Instagram or Snapchat, add a picture with a small description, that’s it nothing more. There is no hassle in uploading the pictures and images of vehicles, just go to PakWheel.com rides section and click on the Post a Ride button and after that only fill the information and hit the post-ride button. Now your vehicle is ready to be viewed by thousands of auto enthusiasts who visit us. Moving onwards, if you are just visiting for the sake of viewing the car pictures or bike pictures, there is a separate option for both of them. There is an option for users to see all vehicles at the same time. However, one can also view his/her desired model separately. Amazing isn’t it.
