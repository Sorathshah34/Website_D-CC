About Car Comparison


Comparisons form a large chunk of thinking/research process we go through before purchasing anything. Since cars are not something, we buy every day; rigorous car comparisons become ever so more important. We at PakWheels.com work hard to bring forth all the car related resource material you need so that you can make an informed buying decision. Similarly to ease out car comparison in Pakistan, we offer our car comparison portal. To compare the cars under your consideration just select the make and model from the drop-down menus and hit the compare button. As of now, you can compare up to three different cars at the same time. Once you are at the comparison sheet, you can further choose the exact variants you are looking to compare which also mean that you can compare multiple variants of the same car. For your added convenience, we pick out the most trending comparisons and list them under the 'Hot Car Comparisons' so that you can compare with just one click! The car comparisons portal compares the cars you select on the following characteristics:


User Ratings/Reviews


We understand that a car is a lot more than just technical specification so to give you a better insight, with just one click you can read/view buyer opinions over the cars you are comparing.


Pricing


Price is a central factor when buying cars in Pakistan and around the world; we know that your selection depends on a car’s price point so you can compare prices of the cars you are comparing side by side in one sheet!


Specifications


In the specifications section of your comparison, you will see exterior dimensions, weight, overall mileage and seating capacity listed side by side of the cars you compare.


Features


As with specifications segment, under features you will see a list of optional/standard equipment available including but not limited to safety features, seats material, navigation, cruise control etc. compared side by side. Once you are done with the comparison, you can express your opinions at the end in the form of comments.
